About Me

I am a Software Architect at Cadence Design Systems, Bangalore, with extensive experience in building SoC Virtual and Hybrid platforms that accelerate early software development and performance tuning.

Over the years I have designed and delivered IP modeling infrastructure, modelled complex IPs such as DMAs, Interconnects and full system integrations. I am well versed in every generation of virtual-platform methodology from C/C++ based SSI (Simulation System Interface) right through to industry-standard SystemC / TLM 2.0.

I have built several transactors bridging industry memories (DDR4/5, LPDDR4/5) with a fast coherent SystemC memory sub-system, a DSI Display Host Controller interface and a CSI-2 Camera device transactor for RTL co-simulation.

Outside work, I love experimenting with modern C++, learning new programming paradigms, writing for my tech blog, and creating simple programs to introduce kids to coding.
